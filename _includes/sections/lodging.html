Accomodation

We know you like to have a good time, without having to worry about transport home. Here are some our suggested places to stay after the reception.

  • Quality Inn Lawrence — University Area is the closest hotel to the venue — it's literally thirty feet from the park (an easy walk).

  • Baymont Inn & Suites Lawrence is just across the street from the venue (just a few minutes' walk).

  • Hampton Inn Lawrence is across the street from the north end of the park (it's a big park — fifteen minutes' walk).

There are plenty of other hotels in Lawrence (including downtown), and Kansas City is only a 45 minute drive from the venue, if you'd prefer to stay there.
