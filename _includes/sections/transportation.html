Travelling to our Wedding

Saint Benedicts Church is located in Chippendale near the Sydney CBD. If you would like to drive to the church, there is limited street parking nearby, otherwise it is a short walk to Broadway Shopping Centre, which has a large carpark. The Grounds of Alexandria has parking available on site.

For those of you driving from the South West, the easiest route will be the taking the M5 until Cleveland Street.

For those of you coming by train, the church is a short walk from Central Station and the reception is nearby Green Square Station.
